Ingredients:

1 C green lentils

1 Package washed lettuce greens

2 T olive oil

1 t balsamic vinegar

Instructions:

Boil lentils 12-14 minutes in 3 C water.
Drain cooked lentils (if needed) and cool in refrigerator 30 minutes.

In serving bowl, add lettuce greens, drizzle with olive oil and viengar and toss.

Top salad with cooked lentils and enjoy!
